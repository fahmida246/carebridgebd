Our vision and mission

Our vision

To be known and valued for providing the highest standard of in-home care service in the community. We strive to create job opportunity for the wellbeing of our society.

Our Mission:

We are committed to providing high quality, client-centered and affordable Home Care and Health Care services to our clients to assist them to lead dignified and independent lives in the comfort and safety of their own homes. Their individual needs are carefully assessed, understood and met through the selective assignment of qualified, trustworthy and compassionate personnel.

Value Statement:

Our vision & mission will be achieved through the application of our core values, which include: Keeping our client’s health, quality of life and well-being, we have designed the way of delivery of services; treating and interacting with our clients with respect, dignity, compassion, empathy, honesty and integrity while recognizing and maintaining confidentiality of client information; showing respect for all cultures, religions, ethnicity, gender and disabilities.
